## Overview
**The Foray Firm** is a family law practice requiring a professional and engaging online presence. I developed a custom WordPress website using **Elementor Pro**, translating a Figma design into a functional and responsive site.

---

## 👨‍💻 Role
**WordPress Developer** (Full Site Development)

---

## 🎯 Goal
1. Transform Figma design into a fully functional website.
2. Ensure fast load times and mobile responsiveness.
3. Optimize SEO and accessibility for a law firm audience.

---

## 💡 Solution
1. **Custom WordPress Development:** Implemented a modular design approach using Elementor Pro.
2. **Performance Optimization:** Optimized images, caching, and clean coding practices.
3. **SEO & Accessibility:** Integrated metadata, semantic HTML, and structured content for improved visibility.

---

## 🚀 Results
- **Faster load speeds** (scoring 90+ on PageSpeed Insights).
- **Seamless user experience** on both desktop and mobile.
- **Improved search rankings**, increasing organic traffic.

---

## ✨ Final Thoughts
- A well-structured law firm website improves credibility and trust.
- Combining WordPress flexibility with Elementor’s design power allows rapid iteration and updates.
